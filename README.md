## About me

I build systems that operate at national scale — not side projects, but production platforms that law enforcement agents, judicial professionals, and thousands of citizens depend on every day.

Based in **Nouakchott, Mauritania**, I work at the intersection of **AI**, **security**, and **government infrastructure**, while conducting independent research on AI models for the **Hassaniya Arabic dialect** — one of the first efforts of its kind for this low-resource language.

- Engineered at **IBTIKAR-Technologies** since 2024
- **M.Sc. Artificial Intelligence** — Université de Nouakchott
- Building what Mauritania's digital future runs on
